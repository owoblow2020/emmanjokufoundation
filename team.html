Our Team

Emma Njoku Esq.

Founder & Chairman, BoT.

Emmanuel Njoku, a dedicated executive from Nigeria, excels in entrepreneurship. With a global business presence, he offers diverse services in healthcare, renewable energy, HR, law, and more. Notably, he chairs the Emma Njoku Foundation, aiding numerous causes. He manages Prezzo Shed Investments Ltd, supporting African healthcare, and holds board positions in various companies.

Mrs. Justina Onyeka Njoku

Co-Founder & Secretary, BOT.

Mrs. Justina Onyeka Njoku, co-founder of the Emma Njoku Foundation, is dedicated to aiding the needy, sick, and elderly. An accountant and MBA holder, she serves as head of the Finance unit, ensuring successful financial management and disbursement for all Foundation activities, showcasing remarkable leadership and humility.

Dr. Albert O. Anusi

General Manager

Dr. Albert Ogbonnia Anusi, a retired banker, holds degrees in Banking and Finance, Management, and Finance. With a notable career spanning public and private sectors, he serves as General Manager at Prezzo Shed Investments Ltd and supervises the Emma Njoku Foundation. An esteemed researcher, mentor, and specialist in human resource management, he's undergone procurement and supply chain training in Luxembourg and Denmark.

Maxwell C. Ezelagu Esq.

Program Manager

Maxwell C. Ezelagu, aka Karlmax, is a versatile individual passionate about complex tasks. As a leader in multiple organizations, he supports innovative ideas, education financing, and humanitarian efforts. With interests spanning entertainment, politics, and community development, he has a 12-year track record of mobilizing resources for communal development and aiding the less privileged.

Sis. Elizabeth Njoku

South-East Coordinator

Sis. Elizabeth Ngozi Njoku is a University of Port-Harcourt Teaching Hospital Certified Community Health Officer. A selfless, dedicated and compassionate servant in God’s vineyard. She is a Reverend Sister of the Catholic Church and has been part of a wide range of charity causes and group philanthropy.

John Iloanusi

Monitoring & Evaluation Officer

John Iloanusi is a seasoned professional with extensive experience in coordinating project monitoring and evaluation, procurement, supply chain management, data analysis, and technical reporting in research, public policy, and public health. He holds a degree in Economics from the University of Abuja, MBA, and certifications in Public Health. John has contributed to donor-funded projects including those by the World Bank, Global Fund & USAID.

Ifeanyi Geraad Ihugba

Programme Officer

Ifeanyi is a Researcher, Project developer, a Digital Marketer, experienced in management; both private and public sector in the fields of Non-Governmental Agencies and oil and gas. He has a master’s degree in International Affairs and Diplomacy; a Post Graduate Diploma in Public Administration, Passionate about research and Project Development.
