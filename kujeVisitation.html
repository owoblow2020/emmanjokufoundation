Event Summary!

Easter Kuje Correctional Center Visitation offered a unique opportunity for the inmates by extending goodwill and support to incarcerated individuals during the Easter season. This initiative embodies the spirit of compassion and renewal, providing a platform for families, volunteers, and community members to visit and engage with inmates in Kuje Correctional Center.
